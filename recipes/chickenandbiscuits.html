Chicken and Biscuits

A tray of finished Chicken and Biscuits.

This recipe is a creamy chicken and vegetable casserole, topped with flaky, golden biscuits. A deliciously delicious combination of vegetables, chicken, bread, and more, this snack will be very snackalicious for anyone who enjoys eating food with chicken or biscuits in it. It will not take long to make, and it is a very good meal. In fact, I would say it's one of the best meals ever.

Ingredients

  • 1 (10.75 ounce) can Campbell's® Condensed Cream of Celery Soup or Campbell's® Condensed 98% Fat Free Cream of Celery Soup
  • 1 (10.75 ounce) can Campbell's® Condensed Cream of Potato Soup
  • 1 cup milk
  • ¼ teaspoon dried thyme leaves, crushed
  • ¼ teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 4 cups cooked cut-up vegetables*
  • 2 cups cubed cooked chicken
  • 1 (7 ounce) can refrigerated buttermilk biscuits

Steps

  1. Mix soups, milk, thyme, black pepper, vegetables and chicken in 3-quart shallow baking dish.
  2. Bake at 400 degrees F for 15 minutes. Stir.
  3. Cut each biscuit into quarters.
  4. Arrange cut biscuits over chicken mixture. Bake 15 minutes or until biscuits are golden.
